05. NOTES & ALERTS

  • BAND C: Already used for lightly licensed, higher powered fixed outdoor systems. Licensing still required for fixed outdoor systems (Ref: IR 2007).
  • WEATHER RADAR: Channels 120-128 are not available on some vendor systems due to 10-minute CAC (Channel Availability Check) restrictions.
  • FUTURE ALLOCATION: Further spectrum allocations unlikely before WRC 2019.
