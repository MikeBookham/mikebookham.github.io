WIDTH RECOMMENDATION STATUS
20 MHz HIGHLY RECOMMENDED Default standard. Maximizes non-overlapping channels.
40 MHz NOT RECOMMENDED Consumes 80% of entire band. High risk of interference (CCI/ACI). Leaves only 1 non-overlapping channel.

05. COMMON INTERFERENCE SOURCES

  • MICROWAVE OVENS: Operate heavily in 2.4GHz. Causes massive packet loss.
  • BLUETOOTH: Frequency hops across the entire band (low power but pervasive).
  • ZIGBEE / IOT: Smart bulbs, sensors often use 2.4GHz (Channels 11-26 Zigbee overlap WiFi 1-13).
  • AV SENDERS: Analog wireless video cameras often jam the entire band.
