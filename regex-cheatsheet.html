01. ANCHORS & BOUNDARIES

PATTERNDESCRIPTIONEXAMPLE
^Start of string (or line in multiline)^Start
$End of string (or line in multiline)End$

02. CHARACTER CLASSES

PATTERNDESCRIPTIONEQUIVALENT
.Any character (except newline)
\dDigit[0-9]
\DNot a digit[^0-9]
\wWord character[a-zA-Z0-9_]
\WNot a word character[^a-zA-Z0-9_]
\sWhitespace (space, tab, newline)[\t\r\n\f]
\SNot whitespace[^\t\r\n\f]
[abc]Character set (match a, b, or c)
[^abc]Negated set (match anything EXCEPT a, b, c)
[a-z]Range (match a through z)

03. QUANTIFIERS

PATTERNDESCRIPTIONMEANING
*0 or more times{0,}
+1 or more times{1,}
?0 or 1 time (Optional){0,1}
{n}Exactly n times
{n,}n or more times
{n,m}Between n and m times
*?Lazy match (match as little as possible)

04. GROUPS & FLAGS

PATTERNDESCRIPTIONEXAMPLE
(abc)Capture group\1 (backreference)
(?:abc)Non-capturing groupCannot reference
(?=abc)Positive Lookahead (Followed by)d(?=r) matches d in dr
(?!abc)Negative Lookahead (Not followed by)d(?!r) matches d in da
gGlobal flag (match all occurrences)/search/g
iCase insensitive flag/Search/i
mMultiline flag (^ and $ match lines)/search/m
